Different Hospitals I Want to Go To

Johns Hopkins

Located in Baltimore, Maryland, this Ivy League school is on the top of my list for attending med school. Because it is Ivy League, it is extreamly expensive, but I am hoping that I will save enough during my 8 years in state. It has an amizing program and they release exeptional doctors from their mold. I want to be one of those great doctors one day.

Tulane

This school is located in New Orleans, Luisiana. I love the south and I would love to go back to the culture and experiance a whole different culture within the southern community. It is my back up plan for if Johns Hopkins falls through. It has a very good program, and they have a good team within the community as a whole and the emergency response team.

Harvard

Another good school located in Cambridge, Massachusetts. It is also Ivy League, but it is not my first choice. It has a lower aceptance rate than Johns Hopkins. They also have a good program but it is also too expensive for me personally.
