Good Schools I am Considering for Each Degree

UNK

Located in Kearney, NE, this school is rated one of the cheapest schools in the nation. The entire costs for the year add up to about $20,000 including tuition, room, food, and miscellaneous. They have many different programs for all kinds of different feilds.

UNO

This school is in Omaha, NE. This school is slightly more expensive than UNK, but only by a couple thousand. This school, in my opinion, has better programs and oppurtunities than either school mentioned here. But it is in a bigger city and may not be for everyone.

UNL

This school is the most expensive of the three($30,000). This school is located in Nebraska's capitol, Lincoln. In a way, that explains why it is so expensive. They have some good programs, but I don't know if dual credit transfers.
